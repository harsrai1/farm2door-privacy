1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our Service:

a. Personal Information

  • Name: To personalize your experience.

  • Delivery address: Including street, city, state, PIN code, and optionally saved addresses with GPS coordinates.
  • Payment details: We may collect limited payment data (like UPI ID, last 4 digits of cards) through secure payment gateways. We do not store full card data on our servers.

b. Location Information

  • GPS coordinates for delivery address selection and map features.
  • Approximate location via IP address for service availability checks.

c. Usage Data

  • Features used, time spent in the App.
  • Interaction logs (like orders placed, items added to cart).
  • Error logs, crash reports, diagnostics.

d. Device Information

  • Device type, model, OS version.
  • Unique device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID).
  • App version.

e. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies or local storage to remember preferences and improve user experience.

We use collected information for purposes including:
