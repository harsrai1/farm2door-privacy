Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Farm2Door ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our mobile application and related services ("Services"). By using Farm2Door, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. What Information We Collect

a. Personal Information you provide:

  • Name, email, phone number (for login/registration via Firebase Authentication)
  • Addresses you enter manually or select on the map
  • Location information you choose to share when using location-based features

b. Automatically collected data:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data (such as screens viewed, crash logs)
  • Approximate or precise location if you grant permission

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Register and manage your account (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Store your delivery addresses securely (using Firebase Database)
  • Enable map-based address selection via Google Maps API
  • Improve and personalize your app experience
  • Communicate with you about orders or updates
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information:

  • With trusted services (e.g. Firebase, Google Maps) for app functionality
  • If required by law or to protect our rights
  • With delivery partners for fulfilling your orders

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

We use Firebase services to securely store your data. We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. Despite our efforts, no system is completely secure. Please keep your login information safe.

5. Your Choices

  • Update or delete your account data via the app or by contacting us
  • Control location permissions in your device settings
  • Opt-out of promotional notifications in app settings

6. Children's Privacy

Farm2Door is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the new policy.

8. Contact Us
